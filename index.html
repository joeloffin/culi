About Culi

Culi is a platform connecting food enthusiasts with extraordinary culinary experiences. We curate exclusive dining events, chef collaborations, and culinary workshops that celebrate the art of food and dining.

Founded in Washington DC, we're dedicated to showcasing the best culinary talent and creating memorable experiences that go beyond traditional dining.

Our mission is to make exceptional dining experiences accessible to everyone, whether you're a local foodie or visiting from out of town.
